We are interested in studying the disease pathology of autism, schizophrenia, dental caries, and periodontitis. By integrating multi-omics data including whole-exome, whole-genome, and epigenetic data, our work aims to develop innovative computational approaches and apply existing analytical tools to uncover new biological insights.
